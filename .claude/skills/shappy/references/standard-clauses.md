## Section II — Scope subsection structure

Every scope subsection follows this pattern:

```
[Letter]. [Service Name] — [Lead narrative sentence describing what OT Law will do.]

[Optional: additional narrative context — 1-2 sentences.]

**Coverage:** (or Per-[X] Package:)
1. [Deliverable 1]
2. [Deliverable 2]
...

***Exclusions:*** [Italicized block listing what's NOT included in this subsection — third-party fees, specific carve-outs, non-guarantee disclaimers for third-party approvals.]
```

Use "will" (committed) for services the client is paying for. Use "may" only for genuinely optional items the client elects to use.

## Appendix A — Standard Terms and Conditions (use verbatim)

### I. Commencement

This engagement shall commence upon the Client's confirmation of the proposal and OT Law's receipt of the agreed fees or any required deposit, if applicable.

### II. Scope of Services

OT Law shall use reasonable professional efforts to provide the legal services described in the proposal. Such services may include the review and assessment of records, contracts, regulatory filings, permits, licenses, property documents, and other relevant records, as well as legal research, analysis of applicable laws and regulations, and the preparation of summaries, memoranda, or reports based on the documents and information made available to OT Law.

The Client agrees to provide complete, accurate, and timely information and documentation necessary for the performance of the Services. OT Law shall be entitled to rely on the information and documents provided by the Client or its representatives without independent verification, unless otherwise expressly agreed. The scope of OT Law's review shall be limited to the documents and information made available to it during the course of this engagement.

The Client's engagement of OT Law is on a non-exclusive basis, and OT Law may provide legal services to other clients, subject to applicable rules on conflict of interest.

### III. Exclusion of Other Legal Services

This engagement is limited to the specific services described in the proposal. Any legal services outside the agreed scope, including additional advisory work, legal research, document preparation or review, regulatory filings, corporate registrations, due diligence reviews, contract drafting, negotiation of agreements, transactional support, representation before courts or government agencies, or other matters not contemplated at the start of this engagement, shall be subject to a separate agreement and billing arrangement.

### IV. Confidentiality

OT Law shall treat as confidential all information and documents provided by the Client in connection with this engagement and shall not disclose such information to any third party, except as may be required by law, by applicable professional obligations, or as necessary for the performance of the Services.

OT Law may disclose such information to its partners, associates, employees, or consultants who are involved in this engagement and who are bound by similar obligations of confidentiality.

The Client likewise agrees to treat as confidential any legal advice, memoranda, reports, or work product prepared by OT Law and shall not disclose or distribute such materials to third parties, except to its officers, directors, employees, advisors, or representatives who require access for legitimate business or transaction-related purposes, or as may be required by law or regulatory authorities.

### V. Conflict of Interest

Based on OT Law's current review, no conflict of interest exists that would prevent it from undertaking this engagement. Should a conflict arise during the course of this engagement, OT Law shall promptly inform the Client and take appropriate action consistent with applicable professional rules.

### VI. Third-Party Reliance

Any advice, memoranda, reports, or work product prepared by OT Law in connection with this engagement are solely for the use and benefit of the Client. No other person or entity may rely on such materials without the prior written consent of OT Law.

### VII. Term

This engagement shall remain in effect until the completion of the Services described in the proposal, unless otherwise agreed by the parties. Should either party decide to discontinue this engagement, reasonable written notice shall be provided. The Client shall remain responsible for payment of legal fees and expenses corresponding to services rendered up to the effective date of such discontinuance.

**Drafting note:** If the matter includes claim windows that extend beyond the Agreement term (e.g., one-year Winner Services claim window reckoned from a trigger date that falls mid-engagement), add a carve-out: "Notwithstanding the foregoing, any claim windows expressly stated in the proposal shall survive and remain enforceable for their stated duration."

### VIII. Non-Waiver

No failure or delay by either party in exercising any right under this engagement shall operate as a waiver of such right.

### IX. Severability

If any provision of this engagement is determined to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.
